# PATCH NOTES v2.1.117 — Living Casino Sector Menu / BET / COPY / GHOST Polish

## ЖИВОЕ КАЗИНО: ring UX

- RMB opens/closes the Living Casino sector ring.
- LMB while the ring is open now only selects the hovered sector and closes the ring.
- LMB no longer activates a sector while the ring is open.
- Closed ring no longer leaves a selected sector panel stuck next to the player.
- Sector cards in the open ring are wider and more UI-like.
- Cooldown text was removed from inside the sector cards.
- Selected sector status is now shown in a HUD plate under dash pips on the right.

## ЖИВОЕ КАЗИНО: BET

- BET now has a visible rolling phase above the player before the result resolves.
- Stake is paid immediately, but payout/loss appears after the roll delay.
- BET roll/result effects are clearer and no longer feel instant.

## ЖИВОЕ КАЗИНО: COPY

- COPY now has its own cooldown feedback and sound.
- COPY now correctly repeats DMG auto-fire, not just one-shot sector actions.
- COPY emits a clear COPY effect when used.

## ЖИВОЕ КАЗИНО: GHOST

- GHOST now has its own sound.
- GHOST uses a dedicated local FX instead of the generic mutation effect.
- Removed the confusing stray/long-line style artifact caused by closed-ring sector connectors.
