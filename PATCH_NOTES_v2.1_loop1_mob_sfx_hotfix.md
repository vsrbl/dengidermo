# PATCH NOTES v2.1 — Loop 1 Mob SFX Hotfix

## Changed
- Removed the confusing one-shot wave-arrival sound from the loop-1 Splitter `SPL SWARM` enemy combo.
- `SPL SWARM` still keeps its gameplay/visual combo behavior, but no longer plays the same audio cue as a director wave.
- `SPL SWARM` no longer spikes the adaptive music chaos layer.

## Why
A loop-1 mob could sound like a strange/unclear special event. The audio cue now stays reserved for clearer wave/director events.
