# v2.1.171 — Living Casino unlock, balance, aim and HUD

- Living Casino starts with LVC only; Control Sparks must be unlocked from WPN choices or another weapon grant.
- Control Sparks deal no damage at base. The first spark-damage upgrade enables damage.
- Reduced base LVC damage, fire rate and acquisition/projectile range.
- Reduced base Control Sparks range; range remains upgradeable.
- LVC range now follows projectile-range upgrades.
- Removed projectile ricochet from Living Casino WPN choices.
- Idle gun indicators are short and rotate slowly around the player; when a target appears, the full line turns smoothly with the barrel and extends only within the weapon range.
- Removed the duplicated compact weapon-slot HUD for Living Casino. The dedicated bottom-right weapon rack remains.
- The SPK HUD row and targeting line appear only after the module is unlocked.
- Updated RU/EN player-facing descriptions.
